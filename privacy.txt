Privacy Policy
==============

What data the app collects
--------------------------

We collect the phone number, name, and profile picture of the registered user and their contacts.

How the app collects data
-------------------------

We use the contacts framework to access the registered user's contact information.

All uses of the data 
--------------------

The purpose of the app is to show users people they can rate. The user information that we store is only used to provide the core functionality of the app.

Third parties
-------------

We do not use any third party APIs or SDKs.

Data retention/deletion policies
--------------------------------

We store user information indefinitely. 

Please contact factheartemail@gmail.com if you would like to delete your data.
